Coloured Mirrored Lenses: Category 3 Lens.

Coloured Mirrored Coatings are a multi layer application that provides 100% protection against Uv Rays and reduces infrared light by 95%. The Colour coated lenses have a 10 layer application compared to the standard 3 layer mirrored lenses.

Amber/Gold Lens: Category 3 Lens.

A Brighter Lens that blocks out blue light & glare, which improves contrast & depth perception. The perfect lens where distances need to be judged. An Ideal lens when acute visual perception and contrast differentiations are required.

Smoke Lens: Category 3 Lens.

A General purpose lens that blocks out glare. Transmits Colours evenly without changing any colour value. The best lens for bright sunny days.

Red Lens: Category 3 Lens.

Helps block out blue light, improving contrast. Red Lenses are soothing on the eyes and are ideal for long periods of wearing.

Blue Lens: Category 3 Lens.

The highest Contrast lens which reduces glare from visible white light, such as light reflected from water and snow.

Yellow (Clear) Lenses / Light Enhancing: Category 0

Reduces Glare, Improves Contrast and preserves sharpness. The yellow lens has a high intensity tint for maximum sight performance during low light conditions. (Heavy Clouds, Snow Falls, Fog or Haze.) The yellow lens enhances contrast by filtering out scattered, out of focus blue light.
