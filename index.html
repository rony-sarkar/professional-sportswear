Amber/Gold Lens:

A Brighter Lens that blocks out blue light & glare, which improves contrast & depth perception. The perfect lens where distances need to be judged. An Ideal lens for Golf, Skiing, Tennis or other sports requiring acute visual perception and contrast differentiations.

Rose / Red Lens:

Blue Lens:
